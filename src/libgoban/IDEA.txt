Goban structure holds five categories:
*grid
*stone
*marker
*label
*highlight
Each category is separate as global (the whole boards) or local (a goban intersection).
The globals categories describes style, stroke and fills patterns, stroke parameters,...
The locals categories describes the presence or absence of each categories:
*grid:NONE|PLAIN|STARPOINT
*stone:NONE|BLACK|WHITE
*marker:NONE|CROSS|TRIANGLE|DIAMOND|PENTAGON_STAR|CIRCLE|SQUARE|BRAND
*highlight:NONE|HL1|....
